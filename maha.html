Welcome to Maharashtra

Explore the rich culture, history, and natural beauty of Maharashtra.

Maharashtra, one of the most vibrant and diverse states in India, is a perfect blend of historical monuments, hill stations, beaches, and cultural richness. Known for iconic landmarks like the Gateway of India in Mumbai, the ancient Ajanta and Ellora caves, and the scenic hill stations of Mahabaleshwar and Lonavala, Maharashtra offers a unique travel experience. Visitors can explore the bustling city of Mumbai, known as the financial capital of India, and its iconic Marine Drive. The state is also home to several forts like Sinhagad and Raigad, and the lush green landscapes of places like Matheran and Alibaug. Maharashtra’s cuisine, including vada pav, pav bhaji, and modaks, adds a flavorful dimension to the experience. Whether you’re seeking adventure, history, or relaxation, Maharashtra is a destination that caters to every traveler’s needs.

Top Attractions in Maharashtra

Gateway of India

A historic monument in Mumbai, built during the British Raj, offering a magnificent view of the Arabian Sea.

Ajanta Caves

A UNESCO World Heritage site known for its rock-cut Buddhist caves and beautiful paintings and sculptures.

Mahabaleshwar

A serene hill station known for its lush greenery, strawberry farms, and panoramic viewpoints.
