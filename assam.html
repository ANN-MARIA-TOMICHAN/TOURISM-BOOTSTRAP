Welcome to Assam

Explore the enchanting beauty of Assam, the land of tea gardens and wildlife.

Assam, located in the northeastern part of India, is a land of lush green landscapes, tea gardens, and rich cultural heritage. Known for its world-famous Assam tea, the state offers a unique blend of nature, wildlife, and history. The Kaziranga National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is home to the endangered one-horned rhinoceros, while the Majuli River Island, the largest river island in the world, is a cultural and spiritual hub. Assam's colorful festivals like Bihu, its diverse wildlife, and the majestic Brahmaputra River make it a captivating destination for travelers seeking natural beauty and authentic experiences.
