Welcome to Telangana Tourism

Explore the beautiful state of Telangana and its main attractions.

Telangana, a state in southern India, is renowned for its rich cultural heritage, historical monuments, and natural beauty. Hyderabad, the capital, is famous for the iconic Charminar, Golconda Fort, and delicious Hyderabadi Biryani. The state boasts serene landscapes, including the picturesque Pochampally village, known for its traditional Ikat weaving, and the tranquil reservoirs and lakes like Hussain Sagar. Telangana is also home to the ancient temples of Bhadrachalam and Warangal Fort, reflecting its deep spiritual and historical roots.
